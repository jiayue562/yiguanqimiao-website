Part Two – Why to learn Wu Xing

Especially the one I’d like to share with you today - the ancient Chinese Wisdom - five basic elements, or you can call it Wu Xing. Do you know what the five elements are?

In Chinese culture, the world is created by five basic elements or Wu Xing. It not only means the five elements that you can see with your eyes, like wood, fire, metal, water, and earth, but also means five types of energy that you can feel. For example, the sun, it’s hot, bright, and shining, so it also belongs to the element of Fire. So do people. People that have this kind of energy are called Fire People.

There are different types of people in this world, someone you like and someone you don’t like, but we need to get along with them. Take Kevin and me for example. Before we get married, we often quarrel, and we used to break up for more than 20 times in one year. Some of you know that. After we learnt Wu Xing together, we realize that it’s not we don’t love each other, but we don’t know each other. He treats me in his own way. He thinks it’s loving. I think it’s hurting. We cannot change others, but we can improve ourselves. Once you changed, you’ll find the other one changed too.

Wu Xing is a tool to help us solve the problem of relationship and it is also a mirror that can help us to know ourselves and each other clearly and deeply. I hope everyone can enjoy perfect relationship in our life.

【Theory】

There are two kinds of relationship in Wu Xing. Generation and Inhibition.

Part One - Generation of Wu Xing

Generation of Wu Xing shows how the five elements generate each other in nature. The cycle outside is the generation of Wu Xing. Wood generates Fire. Fire generates Earth. Earth generates Metal. Metal generates Water. Water generates Wood. This is also confirmed by Discovery. Generation relationship is like mother and son. They feel comfortable with each other, especially the one that be generated.

Part Two – Inhibition of Wu Xing

These five elements not only generate each other but also restrict each other. We call it Inhibition. The star inside is the inhibition of Wu Xing. Metal restricts Wood. Wood restricts Earth. Earth restricts Water. Water restricts Fire. Fire restricts Metal. Inhibition relationship is like enemies. They don’t feel comfortable with each other, especially the one that be restricted.

Part Three – Model of Wu Xing

The five elements and ten relationships are the Model of Wu Xing. First, we need to know our position. And then we need to know how to deal with the other four types, especially the one that restricts you.

Take Wood for example. Wood people are just like wood, growing, developing, new ideas, romantic. So one day a Wood wife wrote a poem, and showed it to her husband – a Metal guy. “Hey, I write a poem. Is it beautiful?” What Metal people focus on? Result, practical use, they are realistic. “Why do you waste time on writing poem? Can I eat it? Can I wear it? Do something useful.” The Wood wife felt miserable by Metal’s restriction.

No matter which kind of people you meet, these people come to your life to remind you that you need to improve yourself, especially when you don’t feel comfortable with these guys. Because you don’t know them, and you don’t know how to deal with them. So we should learn from it. This is our life lesson.

【Recognition】

Part Two, recognition of Wu Xing. In this part, we’ll learn how to recognize these five types of people. I need to mention here that humans are complex. It’s hard to find someone is pure Wood, pure Fire. Normally people have all of the five elements, but they have some preference, eg. They are more like Wood, or more like Fire. It’s relative.

Now let’s look at Wu Xing people.

Part One – Wood

Wood here means all kinds of plants, like trees. When you think about “Wood”, what comes to your mind? What is “Wood” like? What are the characteristics of “Wood”?

The ancient Chinese think “Wood is characterized by bending and straightening”. What does that mean? What is bent? And what is straight? The branches are bent and the trunk is straight. So now we can see the two features of wood.

·Growing. (Learn)

Wood grows upwards. Grow, grow, grow. Up, up, up. Wood people are also like that. They like learning. They know the world by knowledge. They have desire for self-growth, especially for spiritual growth.

·Developing. (Ideas)

The second feature is “Developing”. The branches are bent and develop continuously. Wood people are the same. They like thinking and have many new ideas, no matter whether it’s realistic or unrealistic, normally it’s unrealistic. On the opposite, these people are romantic and talented.

If you fall in love with Wood people, you don’t need to meet them so frequently. Do write poems. They love it.

If you have Wood customers, send them the material before you visit them. They like to know something by book, data and knowledge. Visiting directly is not the way Wood people like.

·Being alone. (Freedom)

Wood people likes to be alone, and don’t feel lonely. They need their own space, and they like freedom.

If you have Wood staff, please respect their features. They don’t like strict management.

External expression: That’s the inner world of Wood people. How to recognize them from outside? Let’s see their external expression.

·Dressing: simple, special (How do they dress?)

·Body language: not too many (Do they have many body languages?)

·Facial expression: not rich (Is their facial expression rich or not rich)

·Speaking speed: medium (Are they speaking fast?)

·Speaking volume: medium (Are they speaking loud?)

·Tone: flat (What about their tone? Flat or high-and-low? Introvert)

Typical Wood person: Faye Wong. She pursuits spiritual growth, does her own thing quietly and doesn’t mind the opinions of others.

Key words: OK. Let’s think of some key words for Wood people? How would you describe Wood people? Learning, romantic, be alone…

Are you Wood people? Do you know Wood people in your life?

Part Two – Fire

What do you think of Fire? What are the features of fire?

Fire is characterized by flaring up.

·Shining (Super Star)

Fire is bright and shining, right? They are super stars! They want to catch your attention all the time. Hey, look at me! I’m here.

So Fire people like to show off or not? Attention, applause. All of you, look at me.

·Heating (Passion)

Fire is hot. Fire people also make you feel hot. They are enthusiastic, full of passion. They act quickly, do a lot of things every day and always full of energy. In Chinese, we say these people are injected by chicken blood. Like a fully charged battery.

Are they confident or not? Very confident.

·Spreading (Active&Influence)

Fire will not stay in one place. They spread all over.

So Fire people are active or not active?

They like to influence others or be influenced?

They like to lead others or follow others?

Part Three – Metal

Metal characterized by clearing and changing.

What do you think of Metal? What are the features of Metal?

·Hard (Control)

Metal is hard and Metal people are also very strong. They are aggressive and like controlling. Everything must be under control. We must have a plan and must follow the schedule.

·Sharp (Direct)

Metal is sharp, and so do Metal people. So do they talk and act directly or indirectly. If you are wrong, they won’t save face for you. Don’t make any excuses. You must finish your job!

Do you think Metal people are cold blooded? Actually not. They don’t mean to hurt you. They just want get the job done.

Do they focus on things or people?

·Shape (Result)

What do you think about Shape? Think about fire, can you make fire into a shape? No. But you can make metal into a shape. Metal people are the same way. They can work under pressure, or even sacrifice their own benefits to achieve a result. They want to get the result. They are realistic.

I give you an example. Let’s suppose that your boss gives you a task to design a marketing event for your customers. The event is over and you report to your boss. You say the event is very successful, we invited 200 customers, but there were more than 300 customers all together. We also had a band performance. Everyone enjoyed themselves. If your boss is a Fire person. He will say, “Good job! Really nice.” But if your boss is a Metal, you’re dead. Why do you think it’s successful? How many name cards do we collect? How many appointments do we make? How many new customers do we get acquaintance? How many contracts do we sign? How many turnovers do we make from the event? Balabalabala……. It’s goanna kill these people. I want the result.

·Facial expression: serious

Part Four – Water

What does water make you think of? What comes to your mind?

Water is characterized by moistening and descending.

·Moistening. (Care)

Water moistens all beings. Water people focus on things or on people. Just like the water, they always try to understand others, care others. If in a team, they are good coordinators. If in your daily life, they are good listeners.

For example, a Fire person, lost her love and called her friend – a Water person. The Fire person complaint, how can he treat me like that, it’s unfair, and I’m so disappointed. The Water person said nothing, but listened. After the Fire person hang up, she went out and had fun. But the Water person started to worry about her. Is she OK now? She must be very sad. Should I call her back and comfort her?

So Do they focus on people or things?

·Descending. (Follow)

Water is always going up or down? There’s an old Chinese saying, Man struggles upwards; water flows downwards.

Do water people like to show off or keep in a low profile?

They’d like to lead others or follow others?

·Hiding (Aimless, Hesitate)

Water won’t spread to other place like what fire does. It prefers to stay in one place peacefully.

Do Water people have specific goals or not? They are aimless. When they talk with you, they may digress from the subject.

It’s very hard for them to make decisions. They always hesitate when facing choices. I can give you an example. We have a Water patient in our hospital. One day she was about to give birth, and was very painful. The doctor asked her whether she needs labor analgesia. She asked, “Does it have any side effects?” The doctor explained to her. She asked again, “Will I be paralyzed? Will I spend the rest of my life in the wheelchair?” The doctor explained that the chance is nearly zero. The patient said, “Forget it. I won’t need it.” So the doctor left. After a few minutes, the patient called the doctor again, “Doctor, I need it! Please do it for me. It’s too hurt.” So the doctor came again and started to sterilize. But before she finished it, the patient said, “I’m sorry, doctor. I need to think again. I haven’t made my mind.” The doctor had to leave again.

Part Five – Earth

There’s a kind of people who are nothing specially, but combined the features of the other four types. They are Earth people.

Let’s see the earth. What comes to your mind?

· Carrying (Take)

The earth carries everything. Earth people are also like that. They can take a lot of things. Normally, they don’t pick work. If someone takes the work, they won’t take it. If no one wants to do it, then they will do it.

· Harvesting (Tolerant)

The earth is our great mother. It breeds everything.

So are Earth people tolerant or not? Are they shrewd or honest?

· Steady (same work, calm)

The earth won’t often move. The Earth people are also very steady. They can do the same work every day for ten years. It’s goanna kill me, but they are ok with it. As well, they are steady in an emergency. They won’t shout and yell like Fire people. They stay calm.
